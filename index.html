What should you be drinking?

Why water

Water is the most thirst quenching liquid known to me. Any kind of drink can fill you, but only water can truly satisfy you.

Studies have shown that people who drink more water have more energy than when they neglect it.

Why can't I drink something else?

  1. alcohol is a diuretic, which means it causes you to have to go to the bathroom more often. This can often lead to dehydration.
  2. Coffee is also diuretic, and can lead to the same symptoms of dehydration like dry mouth and headaches.
  3. Fruit juice can be very sweet, to the point were your body has trouble absorbing the water in it.
  4. tea cause the same symptoms as Coffee.
  5. Soda cause the same symtoms as Coffee and juice.
  6. Energy drinks cause the same symtoms as Coffee and juice.

It's alright to drink the 6 beverages listed above.
just ensure that you are drinking the recommended amount water & that if you're drinking something sweet dilute it a tad bit with water.
